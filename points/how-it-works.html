How it Works

1

Collect Points

Earn points automatically by interacting with the building services. The more you engage, the more you earn!

On-time Rent +100 PTS
Amenity Booking +25 PTS
Event Entry +50 PTS
Building Pulse +10 PTS
2

Unlock Rewards

Browse the 'Ready to Redeem' section. Each reward has a point value. Once you have enough, simply tap redeem to generate your digital voucher.

3

Scan & Enjoy

Take your digital voucher to the concierge or the relevant facility (like the Lobby Cafe) and scan your code to claim your prize!
