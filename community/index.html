Community Guidelines

Be Respectful

Treat all community members with kindness and respect. We have a zero-tolerance policy for harassment or discrimination.

Group Conduct

Keep discussions constructive and on-topic. Avoid spamming or promoting external services without permission.

Event Hosting

All events must be approved by the community board. Ensure you leave the space in the same condition you found it.

Starting a New Group

Propose new groups via the 'New Group' form. Groups must have at least 5 interested members to be officially recognized.

Reporting Issues

Report any violations of these guidelines or maintenance issues through the app's reporting tool.

Questions?

Feel free to get in touch if you have any questions - Our team is always happy to help.
